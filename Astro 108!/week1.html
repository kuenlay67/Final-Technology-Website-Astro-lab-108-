Week 1

Python, NumPy, and Matplotlib

Week 1 Recap

In this lesson, we were introduced to three essential tools used in astronomy and data science: Python, NumPy, and Matplotlib. Python is a beginner‑friendly programming language widely used for scientific computing. NumPy allows us to work efficiently with numerical data arranged in arrays, making calculations much faster and easier. Matplotlib helps us create visualizations so we can interpret data through graphs.

We practiced plotting a straight‑line graph, comparing two functions, creating a bar chart, and making a scatter plot. These foundational skills prepare us to analyze and visualize real astronomical data throughout the course.
